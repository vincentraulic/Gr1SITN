Implementations

Thanks to many of PMO users, there are multiple implementations of the template for easy integration with back-end frameworks. The following are some of them:

  • Persian RTL by Mohammad Sharifi
  • Yii 2 by Tobias Munk
  • Laravel by Arjay Angeles
  • Symfony by Marc Bach
  • Rails gems: PMO2-rails by Nicolas Besnard and lte-rails (using PMO sources) by Carlos at RacketLogger

Note: these implementations are not supported by Almsaeed Studio. However, they do provide a good example of how to integrate PMO into different frameworks. For the latest release of PMO, please visit our repository or website
